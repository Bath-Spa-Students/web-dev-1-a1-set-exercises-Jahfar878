Perfect Steak Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 high-quality steaks (ribeye, sirloin, or fillet)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • Fresh thyme or rosemary sprigs

Instructions:

  1. Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature for about 30 minutes. This ensures even cooking.
  2. Pat the steaks dry with a paper towel and season generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a cast-iron skillet over high heat until it begins to smoke.
  4. Carefully place the steaks in the hot skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or adjust the time for your desired doneness.
  5. Add the butter, garlic, and herbs to the skillet. Tilt the skillet slightly and use a spoon to baste the steaks with the melted butter for an additional minute.
  6. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest on a cutting board for 5-10 minutes before slicing. This helps retain the juices.
  7. Serve the steaks whole or sliced, with your favorite side dishes.
