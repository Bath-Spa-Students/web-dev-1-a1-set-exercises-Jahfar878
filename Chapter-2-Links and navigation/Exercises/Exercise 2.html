Overview

Stranger Things is an American science fiction horror drama television series created by the Duffer Brothers. The show is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, and centers on the investigation into the disappearance of a young boy, the appearance of a girl with supernatural powers, and the mysterious events surrounding the town.

Key Information

Created by: The Duffer Brothers

Genres: Science fiction, Horror, Drama

Original release: July 15, 2016

Characters

Eleven: A young girl with psychokinetic abilities and a mysterious past.

Mike Wheeler: A boy who befriends Eleven and is determined to find his missing friend, Will.

Jim Hopper: The chief of Hawkins Police Department who investigates the strange occurrences in the town.

Joyce Byers: A mother desperately searching for her missing son, Will.
